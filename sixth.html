The Farm Shop


We're Open!

Our farm shop remains open throughout lockdown as an essential food store and we would like to reassure customers that we take your safety very seriously.
Our staff operate in bubbles and we have a dedicated team working around the clock to ensure our supply chain is not interrupted.
Thanks to our on site bakery our shelves will always be filled fresh every day with baked goods and deli items.
Please observe the social distancing measures in place and keep your distance from other shoppers.

Respect what our team are advising: we shall get through this together!
